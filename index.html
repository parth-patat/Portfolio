About Me

I like to think of myself as someone who enjoys solving puzzles, only mine usually come wrapped in code.

With a Master’s in Information Technology from Arizona State University and a Computer Engineering degree from Gujarat Technological University, I’ve built a solid foundation to keep feeding my curiosity for software engineering.

My work so far has taken me through a mix of startups and tech teams, where I have built everything from event-driven backends to full-stack applications that actually scale. I have seen what it takes to make systems reliable under pressure, keep databases efficient, and turn abstract ideas into working software.

The activity I have spent most time on, and enjoyed the most, is brainstorming real-world technical problems and coding my way toward solutions. Whether it is designing an application from scratch or optimizing one to run smoother, I love the process of tinkering, testing, and finally watching things click into place.

At heart, I am a problem solver who happens to use software as my toolkit, always looking for the next challenge to turn into code.
